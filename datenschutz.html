Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

The protection of your personal data is an important concern for us. In this Privacy Policy, we inform you about which personal data we collect when you visit our website, how we use this data, and what rights you have regarding your data.

This website is operated under the name aBest.co and is aimed at users in various countries, particularly in the United States of America and Europe. Therefore, we take into account both European data protection laws, specifically the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), as well as relevant data protection laws in the United States, specifically the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

The use of our website is generally possible without providing personal data. If personal data is collected, this only happens as far as it is technically necessary or if you voluntarily communicate this data to us.

2. Controller

The controller responsible for data processing on this website is:

aBest.co
Website: https://www.abest.co
Email: i@aBest.co

The operator of this website decides on the purposes and means of processing personal data.

3. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Visitors to the website
  • Users of the website's content and services
  • Persons who contact us via email
  • International visitors from various countries

This Privacy Policy applies exclusively to data collected via this website.

4. Definition of Personal Data

Personal data is any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

This includes, for example:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • IP address
  • Location data
  • Technical device information
  • Communication content

5. General Principles of Data Processing

We process personal data only under the following conditions:

  • To provide our website
  • To respond to inquiries
  • To improve our online offering
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To provide information about our products and services

Processing is carried out according to the following principles:

  • Lawfulness
  • Transparency
  • Data minimization
  • Purpose limitation
  • Integrity and confidentiality

6. Data Collection when Visiting the Website

When you access our website, information is automatically recorded by the web server. This data is stored in so-called server log files.

The following information may be recorded:

  • IP address of the visitor
  • Date and time of the request
  • Browser type and browser version
  • Operating system used
  • Referrer URL
  • Host name of the accessing computer
  • Requested files
  • Transferred data volume
  • Server status codes

This data is processed to:

  • Provide the website technically
  • Ensure the security of the website
  • Analyze errors
  • Prevent abuse

This data is not merged with other personal data.

10. Automatic Location Detection

Our website may attempt to determine the approximate location of a visitor based on the IP address. This serves to automatically suggest a suitable language, adapt content regionally, and improve user-friendliness. Location detection is carried out exclusively for technical purposes. This information is generally not stored permanently.

11. Language Selection and Localization

Our website can automatically suggest a language based on the location or browser settings. If the detected language is not available on our website, English is displayed by default. The selected language can be stored locally in the browser to facilitate future visits.

12. Contacting Us via Email

If you contact us by email, the data you transmit will be stored. This can be: name, email address, content of your message, date of the request. This data is used exclusively to process your request. As a rule, it is not passed on to third parties.

13. Cookies

Our website may use cookies. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your end device. Cookies can be used to store language settings, ensure the functioning of the website, and improve user-friendliness. You can deactivate cookies in your browser at any time.

14. Data Security

We use technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data. These include, among other things, a secure server infrastructure, access controls, encryption technologies, and regular security checks. However, no internet transmission can be completely secure.

15. Disclosure of Data

Personal data is only passed on if this is required by law, if it is necessary for the fulfillment of a contract, or if you have expressly consented. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

16. Storage Duration

Personal data is only stored for as long as is necessary for the respective purpose. After the purpose has ceased to exist, the data is deleted, provided there are no legal retention obligations.

17. Rights under the GDPR (EU)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights: right of access, right to rectification, right to erasure, right to restriction of processing, right to data portability, and right to object. To exercise these rights, you can contact us: i@aBest.co

18. Rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a resident of the State of California, you have the following rights: right to know what personal data is being collected, right to access your data, right to delete your data, right to know whether data is being sold, and right to non-discrimination. Requests can be sent to the following email address: i@aBest.co

19. Protection of Minors

Our website is not specifically aimed at children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover that such data has been collected, it will be deleted.

20. Liability for External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. The respective operators are exclusively responsible for the content of these external websites.

21. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to adapt this Privacy Policy to comply with new legal requirements or changes to our website. The current version is available on our website at all times.
